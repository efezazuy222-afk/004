About Wuxi

Located in the southern part of Jiangsu Province, Wuxi is a beautiful city renowned for its stunning landscapes, particularly around Taihu Lake. With a history dating back over 3,000 years, Wuxi is a blend of ancient traditions and modern development.

Known as the "Pearl of Lake Tai," Wuxi offers visitors a unique experience with its classical gardens, historic towns, and delicious local cuisine. Whether you're interested in exploring ancient temples, enjoying scenic views, or experiencing traditional Chinese culture, Wuxi has something for everyone.
